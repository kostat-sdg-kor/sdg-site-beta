Data type

Estimated data by international organizations (Type2)
This is Korean data prepared by international organizations through estimation and modeling.
If there are no national data corresponding to UN SDGs indicators, international data are available for monitoring.
Indicator name

Proportion of members and voting rights of developing countries in international organizations
Definition

The developing country proportion of voting rights and the developing country proportion of membership in international organisations
Data collection

International organizations count the number of developing country members and submit the data to UN-DESA
Organizations

UN-DESA(United Nations-Department of Economic and Social Affairs)
Periodicity

It is different for each United Nation’s organizations
